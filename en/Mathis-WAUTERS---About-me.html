My Profile

Hello, my name is Mathis, I was born in Bordeaux on October 7th 2002. I am 19 years old and I study at CESI engineering school in Bordeaux in computer science.

I am passionate about aviation and digital creations such as photography, video, music or M.A.O. (Computer Assisted Music). I am also very interested in new technologies, computer science and high-tech.

Always looking for novelty and discovery, I like to understand how a mechanism works and try to improve it thanks to science. This is why I am very involved in every project I participate in, where I favour teamwork.

I am open-minded, dynamic, invested, creative and versatile. I am interested in many different subjects and activities.

I am also a sportsman, I practice several sports, soccer, padel, or squash. The one I practice since I was little is tennis.

Finally I love to travel, to discover new landscapes and new cultures.

I hope you will be able to understand my personality through this website, by discovering some of my digital creations.

My experiences

    Personal   |   Professional

Internship in company ATERM (December 2016)

I did an internship in a company in the 9th grade in the design office of the company ATERM. At the end of this internship, I discovered the world of the company until then unknown for me and had the chance to observe the backstage as well as the functioning of this company, which I particularly liked.

Sport

I have been playing tennis since I was 5 years old. I discovered this sport thanks to my father who is a former tennis teacher. He passed on to me his knowledge and his love for tennis. Since then, I haven't stopped and I still take tennis lessons in a club. I also play squash, padel and soccer.

Internship in association, The French Red Cross (November 2017)

Second internship, this time in a charity association. The goal of the internship was to understand how an association works, what we do there, but also what happens there. Thanks to this internship, I became aware of many things and in particular, that many people need help...

Music

Amateur of electronic music and pop, music is my oxygen of life. I create my own Mashup (a mix of two musics). I also practice the piano and the drums. I am self-taught in this field which I like so much.

ERASMUS+ Project Management (2018 - 2020)

For 2 years, I had the opportunity to participate in the ERASMUS+ project. It was a great opportunity! Not only to improve my English, but also to meet new people and work together hand in hand between each country. Erasmus has marked my life as a teenager.

Photography

Photography is for me a whole art, it's also the way to express my thoughts in a picture just by playing with the colors or the framing. In a few days, very quickly, I took a taste for photography and I had a real pleasure to take pictures in the cities which offer so much beauty and emotions retransmitted in a picture.

Polyvalent employee SUPER U (March 2020)

During the COVID-19, I worked at SUPER U in order to answer the lack of employees and the high number of orders. My mission was to do the shopping for the customers in spite of the stock shortage due to the health crisis. This experience taught me to be organized in my work.

Language training in Folkestone, England

For about two weeks, I was able to travel while improving my English. Folkestone, a beautiful little fishing village. Each day was punctuated by two hours of English and three hours of tennis in the afternoon, while staying with a host family. It was a very enriching and fun experience and I was able to visit London and take some nice pictures. This stay allowed me to leave my final year with a B2 level in English.

Assistant delivery and assembly of high-end furniture. (August 2020)

After graduating from high school, I worked for a high-end furniture company, helping with the delivery and assembly of furniture in the customers' homes. It was an interesting job, working in pairs with perfect coordination and organization required to complete our work.

Piloting of two aircraft (Robin DR400, Cesna C-150)

As part of the "brevet d'initiation aéronautique", which I was able to take during my senior year, in 2020, I had the chance to fly two planes for introductory flights included with the BIA. The purpose of the first flight was to observe our instructor and get a feel for the flight controls in cruise. The second flight was to get a feel for the controls and in particular to follow a course. This flight was from Andernos to Biscarosse. It was an extraordinary experience for me.

Carrefour Market polyvalent Employee (March 2021 - Today)

During my studies and having passed the majority, it seemed necessary to me to take charge of my personal needs in order to gain in autonomy.  I worked then 10 hours 45 minutes per week mainly on weekends. I received a training to be a bakery department manager. My mission was to bake bread, prepare desserts and pastries, label them, put them in pockets and on shelves. 
I also worked in the shelves where I was able to sort, advance and put away the products in their place. Finally, I also had a cashier position which allowed me to rediscover and consolidate my customer relationship. Within the framework of this job, I was able to develop my autonomy and my sense of organization in order to serve the customers in the requested time.

Animator Radio Taillan Médoc (RTM)

One day, I came across an article in the local newspaper, which said that the town was looking for hosts for a new show on its airwaves. I immediately contacted my best friend and we immediately agreed, so we applied and were selected as volunteers. That's how we were able to create a show that would bring youth to the radio, by proposing more recent music, technological chronicles or artist discoveries. The show is still broadcast every Saturday from 6:30 to 8:00 p.m. on rtm33 . Even if the beginnings behind a microphone were not so simple, it was a great experience that made us gain in maturity and in eloquence quality.
